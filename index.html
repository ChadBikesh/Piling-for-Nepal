Introduction

Pile Foundation is the technique to transfer the loads of the structures to the deeper and competent soil layer. It plays a significant role to hold the structures securely and rigidly to the original position. In other words, it may be also defined as the foundation system provided for the large and heavy structures with purpose of transferring their static and dynamic loads to the ground level. These types of foundation is very popular to the large commercial buildings, apartments and bridges mostly in the Nepalese scenario.

Existing Method of Pile Construction In Nepal

  • Continuous Flight Auger Drilling (CFA)
  • Auger Drilling with Kelly System
  • Bored-Full-Case Piling Techniques
  • Direct Mud Circulation Piling (DMC)
